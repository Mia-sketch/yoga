gentle hatha yoga
A 60 minute class of poses and slow movement that focus on asana (proper aligment and prosture),pranayama (breath work), and guided mediation to foster your mind and body connection. This class is intended for beginners on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow entergetically through yoga poses. While intended for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to join in this class.
